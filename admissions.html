REDEEMER - FEES STRUCTURE

At Redeemer High School, we believe that every student should have access to a high-quality education, regardless of their financial background. We are committed to keeping our tuition fees as affordable as possible while still providing the resources and support our students need to succeed academically and personally. Our fees structure for both Ordinary Level (O-Level) and Advanced Level (A-Level) education is competitive and transparent. Our tuition fees cover the cost of instruction, textbooks, and most extracurricular activities, with additional fees for specialized programs and services such as transportation, meals, and uniform.

REDEEMER - APPLICATION GUIDELINES

At Redeemer High School, we have made the application process for both Ordinary Level (O-Level) and Advanced Level (A-Level) education as simple and streamlined as possible. Our online application process allows prospective students to easily apply from the comfort of their own homes.

To apply online for O-Level or A-Level education at Redeemer High School, please follow these steps:

  1. Visit our website at (www.redeemerhs.com) and click on the "Apply Now" button. This will take you to our online application portal.
  2. Fill out the online application form, providing accurate and complete information about yourself and your educational background.
  3. Upload all required documents, including transcripts, test scores, and letters of recommendation. These documents will help us evaluate your application and make an admission decision.
  4. Submit your application. Once your application is complete, click the "Submit" button to send it to our admission committee for review.
  5. Track your application status by checking your emails. When our Admission reviews your application, a notification will be send to you in form of an email and sms with either approved or Denied.

Note:

We encourage all prospective students and their families to contact our admission office with any questions or concerns about the application process. We are always here to help and support you as you navigate the application process and prepare for the next phase of your educational journey. At Redeemer High School, we are committed to providing a challenging and engaging academic program to students who are driven to succeed. We look forward to receiving your application and welcoming you to our community.

Redeemer High School

Redeemer High School is a renowned institution committed to providing quality education in Bundibugyo district and beyond. Established in 1980, our school has a rich history of academic excellence and a reputation for producing well-rounded graduates. With a supportive and challenging learning environment, dedicated teachers and staff, and a curriculum designed to meet the needs of students at all levels, we offer a wide range of subjects and extracurricular activities. As a Christian school, we are guided by the principles of faith, hope, and love, equipping students with the values and character traits necessary for a life of purpose and service.

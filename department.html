Departments

At Redeemer High School, we offer diverse departments to provide our students with a comprehensive education. Our science department emphasizes hands-on experiments and research, while our languages department prepares students for a globalized world. Our arts department fosters creativity and self-expression, and our humanities department encourages critical thinking and analysis. Finally, our information technology department equips students with essential digital skills. We are committed to preparing our students for success in college and beyond.

Science Languages Arts & humanities Information Technology

REDEEMER - SCIENCE DEPARTMENT

Our science curriculum is designed to provide students with a strong foundation in the physical, life, and earth sciences. We offer a variety of courses, including biology, chemistry, physics, and environmental science, as well as advanced courses for students who wish to pursue more specialized studies. Our curriculum is aligned with state and national science standards, and we use a variety of instructional methods, including hands-on laboratory work, technology-enhanced learning, and project-based assignments, to engage students and foster critical thinking skills.

Visit Science - lab   →

The science department at Redeemer High School is committed to providing our students with a rigorous and engaging science education that prepares them for success in college and beyond. Our department is staffed by experienced and passionate teachers who are dedicated to inspiring a love of science in our students and helping them develop the skills and knowledge they need to pursue careers in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM).

At Redeemer High School, we believe that science education is essential for preparing our students for success in an ever-changing world. We are proud of the expertise and dedication of our science department, and we are confident that our students will leave our school with the knowledge and skills they need to make a positive impact in the world of science and beyond.

Beyond the classroom, our science department provides numerous opportunities for students to explore science and STEM-related fields. We offer extracurricular activities, such as science clubs and competitions, that allow students to apply their knowledge and skills in real-world contexts. We also provide guidance and support to students who are interested in pursuing science and STEM careers, including advising, mentorship, and networking opportunities.

REDEEMER - LANGUAGES DEPARTMENT

The languages department at Redeemer High School is committed to providing our students with a comprehensive and engaging language education that prepares them for success in an increasingly globalized world. Our department is staffed by experienced and highly qualified teachers who are passionate about language learning and dedicated to helping our students develop the skills they need to communicate effectively in a variety of languages.

Visit Language writers   →

We offer a wide range of language courses, including Spanish, French, German, Mandarin Chinese, and Latin. Our courses are designed to provide students with a strong foundation in grammar, vocabulary, and culture, and to develop their speaking, listening, reading, and writing skills in the target language. We use a variety of instructional methods, including immersion, conversation practice, and technology-enhanced learning, to engage students and foster language acquisition.

Beyond the classroom, our languages department offers numerous opportunities for students to explore language and culture. We organize cultural events, such as food tastings and film screenings, that allow students to learn about and appreciate different cultures. We also provide study abroad and exchange programs that give students the opportunity to immerse themselves in a language and culture outside of the classroom.

At Redeemer High School, we believe that language learning is essential for preparing our students for success in a diverse and interconnected world. We are proud of the expertise and dedication of our languages department, and we are confident that our students will leave our school with the language skills and cultural understanding they need to make a positive impact in their communities and beyond.

We use a variety of instructional methods, including seminar discussions, research projects, and multimedia presentations, to engage students and encourage them to develop their own voice and perspective.

Redeemer High School

Redeemer High School is a renowned institution committed to providing quality education in Bundibugyo district and beyond. Established in 1980, our school has a rich history of academic excellence and a reputation for producing well-rounded graduates. With a supportive and challenging learning environment, dedicated teachers and staff, and a curriculum designed to meet the needs of students at all levels, we offer a wide range of subjects and extracurricular activities. As a Christian school, we are guided by the principles of faith, hope, and love, equipping students with the values and character traits necessary for a life of purpose and service.

SCIENCE LABORATORY

A level students in a chemistry practical

O level students in a biology practical

The science laboratory at Redeemer High School is a state-of-the-science facility equipped with the latest tools and equipment to support the students' scientific inquiry and experimentation. The lab is designed to facilitate hands-on learning experiences and promote critical thinking and problem-solving skills. With a range of advanced technologies, students can explore various scientific disciplines, from chemistry and biology to physics and earth science. The lab is staffed with experienced science teachers who guide the students through the experiments, providing valuable insights and support. The science laboratory at Redeemer High School is an essential resource for any student interested in pursuing a career in science.

Students safety in the Science Laboratory

At Redeemer High School, we prioritize the safety of our students in all aspects of their education, including in our science laboratory. We have implemented numerous measures to ensure the protection of our students while they engage in scientific experiments and investigations. Our students are provided with proper safety gear, including lab coats, gloves, goggles, and closed-toe shoes, to prevent exposure to any hazardous chemicals or materials. The laboratory is also well-ventilated, which prevents the accumulation of dangerous fumes or gases that may be emitted during experiments. Our qualified instructors supervise students at all times and educate them on the proper handling and disposal of hazardous materials. In case of an emergency, our laboratory is equipped with emergency equipment, including fire extinguishers, first-aid kits, and emergency showers that are easily accessible. Additionally, we regularly inspect and maintain our lab equipment and facilities to prevent potential hazards and ensure the safety of our students and staff. We are proud to provide a safe and conducive environment for scientific exploration and learning at Redeemer High School.

COMPUTER LABORATORY

Empowering Students with 21st Century Skills

In today's fast-paced and technology-driven world, computer literacy is essential for success in virtually every industry. That's why Redeemer High School has invested in a state-of-the-art computer laboratory that provides students with the latest tools and resources to develop their technological skills. Equipped with cutting-edge hardware and software, the computer laboratory at Redeemer High School offers students a comprehensive learning experience in various fields such as computer science, programming, and graphic design. The lab is also equipped with high-speed internet access and interactive multimedia tools that enable students to learn in a highly engaging and interactive environment.

The computer laboratory at Redeemer High School is staffed by highly skilled and experienced instructors who are committed to providing students with the highest quality education. Our instructors are not only experts in their respective fields, but they are also passionate about teaching and empowering students with 21st century skills. In the computer laboratory, students have access to a wide range of software and applications that are essential for developing their technological skills. These include programming languages such as Java, Python, and C++, as well as design tools like Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign. Additionally, students can also access online resources such as code repositories, video tutorials, and e-books to enhance their learning experience.

Conducive Computer Lab Rooms

At Redeemer High School, we understand the importance of hands-on learning, and that's why the computer laboratory is designed to provide students with practical experience. Students are encouraged to work on real-world projects and collaborate with their peers to solve complex problems. This not only enhances their technical skills but also fosters teamwork, creativity, and critical thinking. Beyond the classroom, the computer laboratory is also used to facilitate extracurricular activities such as coding clubs, hackathons, and robotics competitions. These activities not only provide students with opportunities to showcase their skills but also enable them to develop leadership, communication, and interpersonal skills.

In conclusion, Redeemer High School's computer laboratory is a valuable asset that provides students with the latest tools and resources to develop their technological skills. Whether you are interested in computer science, programming, or graphic design, the computer laboratory offers a comprehensive learning experience that prepares students for success in the 21st century. We invite you to visit our school and see our computer laboratory in action.

That's why it's essential to have a high-quality dining hall that provides nutritious, tasty, and varied food options.

At Redeemer High School, the dining hall is no exception. Our school takes great pride in its commitment to offering students healthy and delicious meals that cater to a range of dietary needs and preferences. Whether you're a vegetarian, gluten-free, or lactose intolerant, you'll always find something to eat that's both satisfying and nutritious.

Perfect Dinning Hall

The dining hall at Redeemer High School is an essential aspect of student life. We're proud to offer a range of healthy and delicious food options that cater to a variety of dietary needs and preferences. Our commitment to locally sourced and sustainable ingredients ensures that students can enjoy their meals with the knowledge that they're supporting their local community and reducing their environmental impact. And with a warm and welcoming atmosphere, mealtime at Redeemer High School is not just about eating but also about socializing and connecting with others. One of the most significant advantages of our dining hall is the range of food options available. Unlike some schools that offer the same menu every day, our dining hall offers a rotating menu of different dishes, ensuring that students have access to a wide range of healthy and delicious food. Moreover, the food is always fresh and made from scratch, so you can rest assured that you're getting high-quality meals that are both delicious and nutritious. Another benefit of our dining hall is the emphasis on locally sourced and sustainable ingredients. At Redeemer High School, we understand the importance of supporting local farmers and reducing our environmental impact. That's why we source our ingredients locally whenever possible, reducing our carbon footprint and supporting our local community.

Our dining hall is not just a place to eat, but also a hub for socializing and connecting with others. We recognize that mealtime is an essential opportunity for students to relax and connect with their peers. That's why we've created a warm and welcoming atmosphere that encourages conversation and camaraderie. Whether you're catching up with friends or meeting new people, our dining hall is the perfect place to do it.

Redeemer High School

Redeemer High School is a renowned institution committed to providing quality education in Bundibugyo district and beyond. Established in 1980, our school has a rich history of academic excellence and a reputation for producing well-rounded graduates. With a supportive and challenging learning environment, dedicated teachers and staff, and a curriculum designed to meet the needs of students at all levels, we offer a wide range of subjects and extracurricular activities. As a Christian school, we are guided by the principles of faith, hope, and love, equipping students with the values and character traits necessary for a life of purpose and service.

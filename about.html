REDEEMER - SCHOOL PROFILE

Redeemer High School is a co-educational day and boarding school located in Bundibugyo district, in the western region of Uganda. The school was founded in 1980 by a group of community leaders who saw education as a way to help young people in the area rebuild their lives and contribute to the development of their communities. The school has a strong academic program, offering a range of subjects in both science and humanities. The school also places a strong emphasis on community service, with students and teachers regularly engaging in projects to improve the lives of people in the local area. In addition to its academic program, Redeemer High School offers a range of extracurricular activities, including sports, music, drama, and debate. The school has a number of sports teams, including football, basketball, and volleyball, and has competed successfully in local and regional tournaments. Overall, Redeemer High School is a thriving community of learners, teachers, and staff who are dedicated to making a positive impact on the world.

Redeemer High School library

Redeemer High School is committed to providing a safe and supportive environment for its students. The school has a team of experienced and qualified teachers, as well as a range of support staff to help meet the needs of students. The school's facilities include modern classrooms, science labs, a library, and computer lab. The school also has boarding facilities for students who live outside the local area. Redeemer High School is accredited by the Ugandan Ministry of Education and Sports, and has a strong reputation for academic excellence and community service. The school has produced many graduates who have gone on to successful careers in a variety of fields, both in Uganda and internationally. Redeemer High School prides itself on its commitment to excellence, and has a number of initiatives in place to support student learning and development. The school offers a range of academic support programs, including tutoring, study groups, and mentorship opportunities. The school also places a strong emphasis on leadership development, with a number of programs designed to help students develop the skills and qualities needed to become effective leaders. These programs include student government, service clubs, and leadership workshops. Redeemer High School is also committed to using technology to enhance student learning. The school has a well-equipped computer lab and uses a range of digital tools and resources to support teaching and learning.

REDEEMER HIGH SCHOOL LOCATION

Redeemer High school is located in western Uganda in the Bundibugyo District. The school is situated along the Fort Portal - Bundibugyo road, and it is in close proximity to the Bundibugyo Community Worship Center church. If you are a visitor trying to locate the school, you can use these landmarks as reference points. From Fort Portal, head towards Bundibugyo on the Fort Portal - Bundibugyo road. Look out for the Bundibugyo Community Worship Center church, which is a prominent landmark in the area. The school is located near the church, and it should be easy to spot once you reach the vicinity. Alternatively, you can use a navigation app or map to help you find the exact location of the school.

REDEEMER - SCHOOL BACKGROUND

Redeemer High School students in 1980

Redeemer High School was established in 1980 in the western region of Uganda, in Bundibugyo district. At the time of its establishment, Uganda was still recovering from years of political instability and conflict under the rule of Idi Amin, who had been overthrown in 1979. The region around Bundibugyo had been particularly affected by the conflict, with many people displaced and infrastructure damaged or destroyed. It was in this context that the idea for Redeemer High School was born. The school was founded by a group of local community leaders, who saw education as a way to help young people in the area rebuild their lives and contribute to the development of their communities. They were joined by a number of volunteers from other parts of Uganda and from overseas, who provided financial and other support for the school. In its early years, Redeemer High School faced a number of challenges, including limited resources, a lack of qualified teachers, and ongoing political instability in the region. Despite these challenges, the school persevered, and gradually grew in size and reputation.

  1. Today, Redeemer High School is one of the leading schools in the region, with a strong academic program and a commitment to community service. It has produced many graduates who have gone on to successful careers in a variety of fields, including medicine, law, and business.
  2. Throughout its history, Redeemer High School has remained committed to its founding mission of providing quality education to young people in Bundibugyo and beyond. It is a testament to the resilience and determination of the local community, and a source of pride for all who have been involved in its development.
  3. In the early days of Redeemer High School, the school was housed in makeshift buildings with limited resources, but the community's commitment to education was so strong that students continued to attend and learn despite the challenges.
  4. Over time, the school received support from local government officials and NGOs, which helped to improve the infrastructure and increase access to resources. The school's academic program also continued to grow, with the addition of new subjects and extracurricular activities.
  5. Despite the challenges of the past, Redeemer High School has continued to thrive in recent years. The school has expanded its facilities, with new classrooms, dormitories, and sports fields. It has also continued to prioritize community service, with students and teachers regularly engaging in projects to improve the lives of people in the local area.

In the late 1990s, the school faced a new challenge when the region was hit by an outbreak of Ebola virus. The school was closed for several months, and many students and teachers were affected by the outbreak. However, the school worked with local health officials and NGOs to help contain the spread of the virus, and eventually reopened with new health and safety measures in place. Today, Redeemer High School remains a beacon of hope for young people in Bundibugyo and beyond. It is a testament to the power of education to transform lives, and a reminder that even in the most difficult of circumstances, communities can come together to achieve great things.
